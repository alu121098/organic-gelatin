Organic
Gelatin

Our project is an organic gelatin that has enough nutrients to nourish people who suffer from hunger. Our product's priority is to feed as many people as possible since too many people suffer from poor nutrition and our gelatin provides nutrients, as well as vitamins and minerals that will help people achieve good health and be at an appropriate weight.

Proposal for our product
Helping people with low economic resources
Stop Hunger
Make a food that has enough vitamins and minerals for the body

Procedure

-First we will grab our ingredients which are the fruits (strawberry, orange, pineapple, lemon, blueberries), water, a bowl, plastic cups, our vitamins and gelatin.
-We boil a cup of water and when it boils we make the gelatin, which we will add the first fruit with its extra vitamins and minerals.
-We will make the gelatin of the other missing colors.
-We will put the first flavor in our glass.
-We will put it in the freezer and wait for it to harden before adding the next flavor.
-We will go like this with each flavor until we reach the last one.
-Finally we put the gelatin in the refrigerator.
